I am Premankur Banerjee, a PhD researcher in Computer Science at the Haptics, Robotics and Virtual Interactions Lab at the University of Southern California. My research focuses on real-time multisensory multimodal sensing, perception, and rendering feedback for immersive virtual systems.

I design and build hardware-software interfaces at the intersection of Haptics, Robotics, Wearables, and Perception Science to study how haptic and other bodily cues interact with vision and audio during movement or other user actions, and how we can design virtual experiences that feel physically coherent to the user.

Designing the future of touch in virtual and augmented worlds.
